Our Product

The Eye Robot is a visual impairment assistant that utilizes object detection, distance estimation, and text-to-speech (TTS) to help its users navigate through indoor settings.

Two versions of the applications are available for download:
Eye Robot Pro and Eye Robot Lite.

The Eye Robot Pro supports all 3 capabilities and is available for download on iPhone Pro Max models. The Eye Robot Lite only supports 2 capabilities, object detection and TTS, due to technical limitations and is available for download on all other iPhone models.
